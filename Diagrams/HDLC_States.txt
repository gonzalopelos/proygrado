title HDLC Protocol - States

[*] --> Disconected
Disconected --> Disconected : hdlc_read
Disconected --> StartConnection : hdlc_send_sabm\nstart timer
StartConnection --> StartConnection : hdlc_read
StartConnection --> Disconected : time out or\n hdlc_read DM
StartConnection --> Connected : hdlc_read UA
